About us

Welcome to Robotics Club.
Our goal at Robotics Club is to introduce the students
to robotics and programming concepts. The members
of our team share the passion for maths and robots.

Courses

Our courses target students of all ages.
In our classes they can build
the robot collaborating with each other
in teams of 3 or 4 students. We show
to them how to build a robot
and to program it step by step.
The projects are very simple and
we focus more on making
them to think in a creative way.
